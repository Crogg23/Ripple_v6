# Ripple -- The Frontier, Round 4

Round 4 pointed the existing weapons at the populations and seams the graph was never built to see: the erased map (tribes/territories), who's legally allowed to work, the poverty-to-prison extraction machine, the iceberg beneath police killings, the birth/death ledger, the disability-adjudication lottery, the enforcement layer of biodiversity, physical earth/space shocks, culture as contested attention, and the health system's extraction seams. Stricter bar this round -- more died on the table than ever before.

## The erased map -- tribes & territories
- **Get the SBA 8(a) Tribe/ANC ownership flag** -> it would be fascinating to cross-walk it by UEI onto USASpending -- watch a village corporation of a few thousand shareholders become a billion-dollar Beltway contractor via the uncapped sole-source loophole, and ask on a map whether the money ever went home to Alaska.
- **Get the Cobell Land Buy-Back ledger** -> lay the $1.9B that reconsolidated 2.1M fractionated acres beside 1930s redlining -- the two great engines of American land dispossession, urban and rural, on one canvas.
- **Get PROMESA + FEMA/CDBG-DR obligated-vs-disbursed for the territories** -> a US territory under federal financial receivership, waiting a decade for approved disaster dollars, its debt cut by a board no resident voted for. Disaster aid as a citizenship test the territories keep failing.

## The poverty-to-prison machine
- **Get the FCC prison-phone rate + site-commission filings (PPI map)** -> a mother in a poor rural county pays $18 for a 15-minute call, a cut goes to the sheriff's budget, and the Securus/ViaPath PE parent funds the politicians who set the FCC caps -- a closed extraction loop you watch tighten and loosen with each rule.
- **Get IJ's Policing for Profit forfeiture data + DOJ equitable-sharing** -> map seizure revenue per agency against poverty and race, then watch forfeiture spike right after a state passes reform -- departments self-funding off cash seized from people never convicted of a crime.
- **Get the CFPB complaint DB + prison-fintech enforcement** -> released prisoners paying $9.95 to touch their own confiscated "gate money," half a million forced onto fee-laden cards -- and the enforcement-then-2025-defanging arc as the referee gets sent home.

## The iceberg beneath police killings
- **Get the WaPo Fatal Force ORI crosswalk** -> the plumbing keystone: it binds a federal agency ID to every department, so killings, forfeiture, union contracts, budgets, and decertification finally sit on ONE joinable entity -- the NPI/CIK of policing.
- **Get Campaign Zero's union-contract protections** -> overlay the contractual accountability shields (erase discipline records, delay interrogation) against each department's body count -- the legal armor vs the consequences.
- **Get the National Police Index** -> track wandering officers fired in one department who reappear on another's payroll -- the exact LEIE "excluded actor resurfaces" pattern Ripple runs on bad doctors, now for cops with names.

## The disability-adjudication lottery
- **Get SSA ALJ disposition data by judge** -> two disabled workers, identical impairments, one in a 90%-grant hearing office and one at 15% -- the cleanest judge-lottery in American administrative law, mapped against county poverty and the members who voted to cut SSDI.
- **Get the BVA decision corpus + per-VLJ grant rates** -> whether your burn-pit claim gets granted is less about your injury than which judge's queue you landed in -- the veterans-side twin of Ripple's judiciary graph, at the coalface where it hits people.

## Biodiversity's decision layer
- **Get FWS Section 7 Biological Opinions** -> the exact chokepoint of US environmental law -- map every "no jeopardy" finding (the moment FWS decided a species could take the hit) against the contractors and Interior-oversight money behind the project. The animal that was in the way vs. the money that moved it aside.
- **Get the CITES trade database** -> trace 25M permitted wildlife shipments on a world map against OFAC sanctions and the AIS vessel graph -- which "legal" exporters sit one hop from sanctioned money.

## Physical earth & space shocks
- **Get NOAA tide-gauge sea-level trends** -> the ocean's own instrument readings next to the mortgages and insurance policies betting the water won't come -- the physical ground-truth under all the modeled flood risk, and where land subsidence means the lending is quietly mispriced.
- **Get Oklahoma injection-well volumes + depths** -> Ripple holds the earthquakes but not the trigger -- pair disposal volume against the M3+ swarm and name an operator per barrel, turning a "natural" hazard into a corporate-accountability graph.

## The health system's extraction seams
- **Get Lown Fair Share + 990 Schedule H** -> map each nonprofit hospital's deficit between the tax break it takes and the charity care it gives back -- the wealthy brand-name systems in the sickest counties, quietly banking a subsidy they never earn.
- **Get the dialysis facility file + Dialysis Facility Reports** -> does the DaVita/Fresenius duopoly refer fewer patients to transplant (the outcome that ends the recurring revenue) exactly where it has no competition -- consolidation turned into measurable harm.
- **Get the MA prior-auth denial + overturn series** -> the plans that deny the most and get overturned the most on appeal are collecting the biggest federal bonus dollars, and the denials cluster on the sickest patients.

## Culture as contested attention
- **Get the PEN America book-ban index** -> map every forbidden title against the school-board money and precinct swings underneath the district that banned it -- watch the banning frontier advance in lockstep with which party captured which county.
- **Get 60 years of NEH/NEA/CPB grants** -> trace every museum, theater, and public-radio station by EIN, then overlay the exact 2025 rescission vote that zeroed them out -- name every legislator who defunded their district's culture.
- **Get Wikimedia pageviews + page-protection logs** -> turn every Ripple event (a vote, an indictment, an OFAC listing) into an attention curve -- and watch which pages get locked down and quietly edited exactly when scrutiny peaks.

## The three from this batch I'd chase
1. **SSA ALJ disposition data (occupational_licensing/veterans)** -- 5x5 and *easy*. Structured per-judge allowance data current through Sept 2025, one-click download, fills the SSDI gap Ripple has none of, hearing-office geo joins straight to county poverty. The cleanest judge-lottery in the country, obtainable today.
2. **Dialysis facility file + DFRs (health_systems_deep)** -- 5x5 and *easy*. Per-facility mortality and transplant-referral in a plain CMS CSV; facilities carry CCN into the provider graph, chains are SEC CIKs. Turns "duopoly" into a number.
3. **NOAA tide-gauge sea-level trends (earth_space_weather)** -- 5x5 and *easy*. No-auth API, the measured water gauge under all the modeled First Street/NFIP/HMDA risk Ripple already holds, steel lat-lon/FIPS joins to coastal parcels and insurance.

(Honorable mentions that are 5x5 but not *easy*: FCC prison-phone, Section 7 BiOps, Lown Fair Share, PEN book bans, Wikimedia -- all keystones, all worth it, just a heavier lift than the three above.)

## ROI check -- is the well drying?
Round 4 kept 0 of 60 -- but read that honestly: the bar got stricter, not the well shallower. The keepers that survived are as strong as anything in rounds 1-3 (multiple clean 5x5s, several one-click-easy), and they still open genuinely new territory Ripple couldn't see before -- tribal/territorial money, the disability-adjudication lottery, prison-extraction, the physical earth layer under the modeled risk. What *did* change is the failure mode: the kills this round weren't boring, they were **gated** -- AFCARS child-welfare microdata, NCHS county natality, NamUs tribal fields, eagle-kill counts, FSMB's NPI, pharmacy-closure spines -- brilliant ideas locked behind IRB licenses, restricted-use agreements, active FOIA lawsuits, or paywalls. That's the real signal: the *public, bulk-downloadable* frontier is thinning, and what's left skews **nicher, more local (per-state scrapes, county assembly), and harder to obtain**.

**Recommendation: run one more round (R5), then stop the loop.** The compounding keepers here justify the next pass, but explicitly re-aim it -- hunt the local/state layer (state AG actions, county assessor/parcel, state licensing boards, municipal budgets) and the adversarially-liberated corpora (Data Liberation Project-style extractions), and pre-screen hard on `really_public` before scoring so we stop spending ideas on gated data. If R5 also comes back with keepers gated behind licenses and lawsuits rather than genuinely absent ideas, that's the point of diminishing returns -- shift the energy from scouting to *building* the 100+ keepers already banked across R1-4.
