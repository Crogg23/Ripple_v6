# Ripple -- The Frontier, Round 3

Rounds 1 and 2 built the domestic spine. Round 3 leaves the country, jumps forward in time, and walks into the rooms with the lights off -- the offshore ownership webs, the climate future landing on old injustice, the machine that watches, and the money nobody's supposed to trace.

## The offshore world

- **Get OpenSanctions** -> it would be fascinating to see the global kleptocrat/PEP graph laid over our USASpending contractors and FARA lobbyists on a world map -- suddenly "a company won a contract" becomes "a company owned by a sanctioned oligarch's cousin won a contract." This is the entity-resolution keystone that makes every other offshore set joinable.
- **Get UK Companies House PSC** -> it would be fascinating to trace beneficial ownership through British shell wrappers as a network -- which US public companies and sanctioned individuals surface as the real human "person with significant control" behind a BVI shell registered in London. The only large free register that names the humans.
- **Get Global Fishing Watch encounters** -> it would be haunting to watch, on a map over time, the offshore rendezvous where a gone-dark sanctioned tanker meets a clean one just outside territorial waters -- the exact spot where cargo gets laundered, joined to our vessels by IMO.
- **Get Paris MOU ship detentions** -> it would be sharp to see detentions-per-vessel over time next to sanction dates -- the decrepit, flag-hopping "dark fleet" rustbuckets that keep moving sanctioned oil, revealed by their own inspection record.

## The map of what's coming

- **Get First Street climate risk** -> it would be devastating to see 30-year forward flood/fire/heat/air risk mapped straight onto the 1930s HOLC redlining lines -- a straight line from Depression-era discrimination to who bakes and floods first in 2055.
- **Get Climate Impact Lab county mortality** -> it would be grim to lay 2050 projected heat-death per 100k onto the same counties already carrying our overdose, incarceration, and provider-desert data -- future harm settling exactly where past harm already sits.
- **Get WRI Aqueduct water futures** -> it would be striking to slide 2030/2050 water-stress projections under today's data-center and chip-fab buildout -- the map lighting up where thirsty capital is pouring into basins the models say will run dry.
- **Get Climate Central's rescued Billion-Dollar Disasters** -> it would be pointed to run rising climate losses state-by-state against who votes against climate money and gets bailed out anyway -- and the dataset itself was killed by the government in 2025 and rescued by a nonprofit, so the censorship is part of the story.

## The machine that watches

- **Get EFF Atlas of Surveillance** -> it would be chilling to lay the agency-by-agency map of who runs facial rec, ShotSpotter, and predictive policing over redlining grades and police-killings -- the watching machine installed exactly where the redlining ink dried 90 years ago.
- **Get the leaked ShotSpotter sensor locations** -> it would be stark to plot 25,000 actual gunshot-microphone lat-lons (locations the police themselves don't have) against Census race by block -- an acoustic net strung surgically over Black and Latino neighborhoods.
- **Get the Palantir deployment corpus** -> it would be weird and damning to draw one company's tendrils agency by agency -- ICE, IRS, fusion centers, local PDs -- against its own contracts and donations we already hold. One vendor quietly becoming the connective tissue of the entire surveillance state.

## Money with the lights off

- **Get the ICIJ Offshore Leaks graph** -> it would be fascinating to match our donors, contractors, sanctioned parties, and excluded doctors by name against the Panama/Pandora/Paradise officer graph -- who writing checks in the open is also the human hiding behind a BVI shell.
- **Get FinCEN SAR Stats + all-cash real-estate GTO counties** -> it would be revealing to overlay the government's own laundering heat-map onto redlining and donor geography -- where anonymous shell cash pours into luxury condos, and whether it sits atop the neighborhoods redlining hollowed out.
- **Get Wyoming/Nevada/Delaware registered-agent data** -> it would be sharp to cluster the single strip-mall addresses in Cheyenne and Wilmington fronting thousands of anonymous LLCs -- and see how many of those shells reach into federal contracts and campaign money.

## The body as a market

- **Get the OPTN/SRTR transplant registry** -> it would be haunting to map waitlist mortality -- deaths per 100 years of waiting -- by OPO territory against race, poverty, and redlining. Whether you get an organ before you die is decided by which service-area line your ZIP falls on, and those lines trace the redlined cities.
- **Get the FDA plasma-center registry** -> it would be grim to map every paid-plasma center against tract poverty and layoffs -- the US supplies 70% of the world's plasma, harvested from the veins of the poorest, and the centers bloom exactly where the jobs die.
- **Get March of Dimes maternity deserts (AHRF)** -> it would be devastating to animate OB-ward closures county by county over the last decade against race and Medicaid status -- birthing people pushed hours from care, along the same map as every other American harm.

## Public billions, private spectacle

- **Get stadium bond issuances (EMMA subset + Brookings)** -> it would be striking to turn every publicly-financed stadium into a debt object -- 30 years of a city's school and water money -- set against the team owner's donations and the members who carved out the tax-exempt bonds. Subsidy flows one way, political money flows back.
- **Get state cannabis license + sales data** -> it would be haunting to plot dispensary lat-lons (and the "social-equity" licenses meant to repair the drug war) over HOLC redlining and incarceration -- whether legal weed lands in, or skips, the neighborhoods it was supposed to make whole.
- **Get Texas-style lottery retailer + sales data** -> it would be devastating to map ticket sales per capita by ZIP against income and redlining -- the purest regressive-tax map in the country, the machines clustered where the money's thinnest, funding an "education" promise that just backfills the general budget.

## The grid beneath

- **Get EIA-861 utility service-territory polygons** -> it would be revealing to draw each utility's monopoly territory as an actual shape over redlining, race, and county health -- which neighborhoods got carved into whose monopoly, and whether the redlined side pays more and sits nearer the dirty plants.
- **Get FERC Form 1 utility financials** -> it would be sharp to line up each utility's guaranteed return-on-equity against its own political spend and the rate hikes it won -- regulatory capture measured in dollars-per-donation for the first time.
- **Get LBNL's interconnection queue** -> it would be fascinating to map the 2+ terawatts of solar and storage stuck waiting to connect against who owns the wires -- the ghost grid that never got built, and which incumbents benefit from the gridlock.

## The AI boom's physical footprint

- **Get the BIS Entity List** -> it would be pointed to trend who gets cut off from US chips over time against the China-hawk bills and donor clusters that precede each wave of designations -- the policy-enforcement heartbeat nobody trends against the politics driving it.
- **Get TeleGeography's submarine cables** -> it would be novel to draw the five companies that own the internet's undersea spine against our IMO vessel tracks -- which cable-repair ships loiter near a landing point weeks before an outage, in sanctioned or contested waters.
- **Get Epoch AI's model/compute data** -> it would be striking to turn the abstract "scaling curve" physical -- this many exaFLOP = this many GPUs = this much county power -- and see which frontier models would be born on the wrong side of a chip ban.

## The influence endowment

- **Get DOE Section 117 foreign university gifts** -> it would be striking to map Qatar, China, and Gulf money flowing into named campuses against those same schools' policy-center output and NIH grants -- the foreign-influence-into-the-academy pipeline on one timeline.
- **Get the IRS DAF filings (Schedule D)** -> it would be revealing to trace the washing machine -- money into Fidelity Charitable or DonorsTrust, anonymized, out to advocacy groups -- timed against election cycles. The layer where the donor's name is deliberately erased, finally quantified.
- **Get IRS 527 filings** -> it would be fascinating to overlay the state-level political money that files with the IRS instead of the FEC onto our federal graph -- the other half of organized political money, living in the tax-agency's shadow.

## What they won't show us

- **Get the FACA advisory-committee database** -> it would be sharp to map who from which company sits on which agency's regulatory panel against our pharma-payments and lobbying graph -- catching the exact person advising FDA on a drug class while their employer cuts the checks. The org-chart of regulatory capture.
- **Get OSC/MSPB whistleblower + retaliation records** -> it would be revealing to watch retaliation-case volume spike at an agency before the IG report before the scandal -- the earliest tremor, stacked on top of the oversight layer as a three-stage early-warning system.

## The three from this batch I'd chase

1. **OpenSanctions** -- the cheapest, highest-leverage grab in the batch: it turns every US-side name we already hold into a node in a global ownership graph, and nothing else offshore joins cleanly without it.
2. **EFF Atlas of Surveillance** -- a clean bulk download that instantly overlays the entire watching-machine deployment map onto the redlining and police-killing data we already own, with no partner source needed.
3. **CMS Hospice "All Owners" file** -- a free CSV with explicit PE/REIT flags that closes the end-of-life revenue loop the moment it lands, since we already hold the nursing-home ownership graph the same funds control.
