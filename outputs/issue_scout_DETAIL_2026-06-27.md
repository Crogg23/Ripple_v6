  - Unit: One presidential nomination (PN citation, e.g. PN1064) sent to the Senate; sub-resources = one nominee, one committee referral, one Senate/Presidential action, one hearing. · Volume: ~1,000-3,000 nomination records per Congress (2-yr); thousands of nominees/actions sub-records.
