# Ripple -- The Frontier, Round 2

Round 1 mapped the present. Round 2 goes down and back: individual humans of 1850, the ground literally sinking, the trials that went dark, the ambulance that came too late, the deed under the oil well. Deeper, stranger, and every layer snaps onto what you already hold.

## The deep past

- **Get IPUMS full-count census (every person, 1850-1940)** -> it would be fascinating to lay the actual households of 1860 -- who owned whom, who couldn't read -- against today's overdose and incarceration maps -- the counties with the most enslaved people are often the exact ones bleeding now, in individual records, not vibes.
- **Get Chronicling America (20M newspaper pages, 1770-1963)** -> it would be haunting to trace a family name or a lynching from the front page where it first appeared to the modern trail -- the contemporaneous voice of every town, on the same timeline as its outcomes.
- **Get SlaveVoyages + Freedmen's Bureau records** -> it would be moving to bookend slavery with data: the voyages that delivered people to a port, then the ledgers of those same people building free lives -- against 1860 census and today's Black-Belt county maps.
- **Get CDC WONDER mortality (county cause-of-death, 1968-2016)** -> it would be revealing to run the deaths-of-despair curve 50 years backward -- which counties were dying of cirrhosis and "accidental poisoning" in the 1970s, long before opioids had a name.

## Follow the science, follow the money

- **Get the FDA Orange Book (patent-to-drug-product links)** -> it would be damning to watch a drug's price climb as a staircase -- every new patent listing a step up, a delay in generics -- the auditable chain behind "why is this $10k."
- **Get USPTO PatentsView (every patent since 1790, disambiguated assignees)** -> it would be fascinating to watch 19th-century inventors fossilize into today's conglomerates on a map, and to ask how much taxpayer-funded invention ends up owned by pharma and defense.
- **Get ClinicalTrials.gov + the FDAAA TrialsTracker** -> it would be chilling to map, by sponsor and drug, the trials that quietly went dark next to the marketing money that flowed to prescribers for the same pills.
- **Get OpenAlex + Unpaywall (the citation supergraph)** -> it would be pointed to map, by funder and country, how much publicly funded science sits behind a paywall -- public money, private lock.

## The money behind the money

- **Get FFIEC Summary of Deposits (branch deposits, geocoded, back to 1994)** -> it would be visceral to lay the bank-branch money map straight over the 1930s redlining polygons -- watch the branches thin out of the exact blocks the government red-lined in 1935.
- **Get MSRB EMMA muni disclosures** -> it would be revealing to watch a town's bond market flash red -- downgrades, missed payments -- years before the human crisis shows up in the mortality stats. Fiscal collapse as an early warning for social collapse.
- **Get SEC Form 4 insider trades + 13F institutional holdings** -> it would be sharp to line up executives dumping their own stock against a contract loss or sanctions hit weeks later -- the "they knew" pattern, made queryable across the whole market.
- **Get the Public Plans Database (pension funded-status)** -> it would be quietly infuriating to trace teachers' underfunded retirement money into the same PE firms that own the nursing homes those teachers will die in -- a closed loop nobody draws.

## Where the bodies fall

- **Get NVDRS (every violent death as a documented story)** -> it would be devastating to watch "recent eviction" and "recent job loss" flags cluster inside the same 1930s red polygons where overdose and police killings already cluster -- the circumstance of death, block by block.
- **Get NEMSIS (60M ambulance runs a year)** -> it would be chilling to show the gap between an overdose and a corpse is often just how long the ambulance took -- and that gap tracks race and redlining lines.
- **Get daily census-tract PM2.5 surfaces** -> it would be haunting to animate 20 years of pollution breathing in sync with cardiovascular death, both inside the same red lines -- the air people were assigned to breathe.
- **Get FARS joined to recalls + VIN body-type** -> it would be damning to map pedestrian deaths against the rise of tall-hood trucks -- road death as a product-design choice, not an accident.

## Who owns the ground

- **Get DOI Natural Resources Revenue (ONRR royalties by company)** -> it would be fascinating to map which firms extract the most from public land, county by county, against how much those same firms donated to the senators who keep royalty rates frozen at 1920s levels. Public dirt, private profit.
- **Get USGS NWIS groundwater levels** -> it would be haunting to animate the water table dropping 40 years under the Ogallala and Central Valley, overlaid on foreign-owned farmland drawing it down -- aquifers mined dry beneath land the public doesn't own.
- **Get BLM mining claims (royalty-free, 1872 Mining Law)** -> it would be pointed to watch who rushed to lock up public lithium and uranium rights for free right before the EV boom -- a 150-year giveaway and the money keeping it alive.
- **Get the BLM General Land Office patents (the original American land grab)** -> it would be striking to map every 160-acre parcel handed to a named person, then trace it forward into today's mineral rights -- including the ground promised to freed people and quietly rerouted.

## The narrative machine

- **Get FCC political ad files (station-level buys)** -> it would be fascinating to watch a PAC dollar leave the FEC filing and reappear as airtime in the exact media markets a race was won or lost -- the last mile of political money.
- **Get FCC broadcast ownership (station -> parent)** -> it would be revealing to resolve which parents (Sinclair, Nexstar) own which local megaphones, then ask whether the companies selling political airtime also donate to the candidates buying it.
- **Get Medill news-desert data + Media Cloud coverage** -> it would be chilling to map the counties where local reporting died, then watch paid political advertising and uncontested elections rush into the vacuum.
- **Get the EU DSA takedown database (billions of moderation decisions)** -> it would be striking to watch content-suppression spike by category in the weeks around elections and geopolitical flashpoints, at planetary scale.

## The detention business

- **Get EOIR immigration-court cases + ICE detention stays** -> it would be devastating to show a case's asylum odds swing from 3% to 90% purely on which courtroom the bus stopped at -- and that those courtrooms cluster inside private-prison towns whose operators fund the appropriators.
- **Get the 287(g) roster (deputized sheriffs)** -> it would be pointed to map which sheriffs signed up to enforce immigration law against their county's vote margin and police-killings rate -- the deputized-county network overlapping exactly where you already flag police violence.
- **Get CBP/IOM border-death points** -> it would be brutal to plot geolocated migrant deaths against every new mile of wall -- the funnel effect made visible, bodies pushed deeper into the desert with each barrier.
- **Get ORR child-placement geography** -> it would be quietly alarming to overlay where 448,000 migrant kids were placed against the 2025 rule that reopened sponsor addresses to ICE -- a child-welfare list turned into a target list.

## The strange & the real

- **Get the FCC broadband map (address-level served/unserved)** -> it would be moral to snap the digital redline onto the 1936 mortgage redline, then follow the BEAD subsidy dollars to see if the money reaches those blocks or routes around them.
- **Get InSAR land-subsidence rasters** -> it would be haunting to animate the ground literally sinking millimeters a year over pumped-out aquifers, with pipelines and wells buckling on top -- slow-motion theft of a shared resource, made into a moving image.
- **Get the Census of Governments special-district files** -> it would be revealing to map the invisible fourth branch -- 38,000 water/fire/mosquito districts that tax and bond with no ballot-box visibility -- against the places that also over-incarcerate.
- **Get FRA grade-crossing incidents (geocoded, by warning device)** -> it would be quietly damning to color each deadly crossing by whether it got modern gates or just a wooden crossbuck -- the geography of the cheap crossing.

## The three from this batch I'd chase

1. **IPUMS full-count historical census** -- the keystone deep-time spine: 650M individual humans, 1850-1940, keyed straight to modern FIPS, the substrate that lets you say "this county never recovered" with records instead of vibes.
2. **FFIEC Summary of Deposits** -- easy, free, geocoded, and the single most visceral before/after in American finance: the bank-money map laid directly over the redlining map.
3. **FDA Orange Book** -- a free download that turns a hospital's mystery sticker price into an auditable patent staircase, riding entirely on infrastructure (Drugs@FDA, price transparency, Open Payments) you already own.
