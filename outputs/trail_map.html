◢ The corkboard

Every card is a public dataset. A string between two cards means they share an identifier — the same EIN, the same county FIPS, the same court docket. Pull a string and you can actually join those two datasets.

The rarer the shared key, the stronger the lead: an EIN ties two records to one exact business; a ZIP just means "same neighborhood." Bright Steel cards are your most trustworthy starting points.

Click a card and the board re-pins around it — that card moves to center and its strongest connections fan out, grouped by the key they share. Each card you open drops a breadcrumb up top; click back to retrace or branch.

Found one worth chasing? Pin it (★) to your Pull List — your evidence pile, exportable as TSV or JSON.
